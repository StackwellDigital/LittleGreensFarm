Our story

Started in a
garage. Grown with
intention.

Little Greens Farm started when we couldn't find fresh, quality microgreens at our local market. So we started growing our own — and quickly realized we had way more than we could eat. Now we grow for restaurants, farmers markets, and households across BC who believe food should be fresh, local, and alive.

Sarah & Marcus, Founders
